# Process Automation - Metrics and Results

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Before | After | Improvement |
|--------|--------|-------|-------------|
| Account setup time per new hire | 45+ minutes | Under 5 minutes | 89% reduction |
| Data entry errors | 3-5 per month | Near zero | ~100% reduction |
| Time to full system access | 1-3 days | Same day | 1-3 days saved |
| Manual steps per onboarding | 7 | 2 (create record + verify) | 71% fewer steps |
| Identifier duplicates | 2-3 per quarter | Zero | 100% elimination |

## ROI Estimate
- Average 20 new hires per month
- 40 minutes saved per hire = 13+ hours saved monthly
- Reduced error remediation = 5+ hours saved monthly
- Total: ~18 hours per month freed up for higher-value HR work

## Compliance Impact
- Consistent provisioning eliminated access gaps
- Automated audit trail for every account created
- Role-based access applied uniformly from day one
- Reduced risk of unauthorized access during onboarding window
